Services

I offer a variety of data science and machine learning services, including:

Machine Learning Model Development

Develop machine learning models that can be used to make predictions, classify data, or cluster data. These models can be used to improve your business processes, such as customer segmentation, fraud detection, and product recommendation.

Model Deployment and Maintenance

Deploy your machine learning models to production and monitor their performance. I can also help you make changes to your models as your data changes.

Data Collection and Analysis

Collect and analyze data from a variety of sources, including surveys, social media, and customer transactions. I can then use this data to identify trends, patterns, and insights that can help you improve your business.

Data Visualization

Create data visualizations that are clear, concise, and engaging. These visualizations can be used to communicate your data findings to stakeholders, customers, and other audiences.

Computer Vision

Develop and deploy computer vision applications. These applications can be used for a variety of purposes, such as facial recognition, object detection, and scene classification.

Consulting
